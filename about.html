Founded in 2016 and based in Mauritius, SimplissimA is an international research and education institute that is committed to advancing science, medicine and public health. SimplissimA is a private initiative entrusted with five core missions of public interest – research, education, the health of populations, innovation development, and knowledge transfer.

Our mission is to bridging health

Like the little prince of Antoine de Saint-Exupéry, we use a basic drawing to reveal the full essence of our mission. The “bridge” we build at SimplissimA is to establish the link, which currently lacks between traditional medicine and innovative science.

Our vision is to promote One Health

Like the little prince of Antoine de Saint-Exupéry, we realize the importance of responsibility. We work to bring health to all, in a universal continuity, irrespectively of money or geography, and in the consideration of the health of animals and the environment.

Our value is the quality of observations

Like the little prince of Antoine de Saint-Exupéry, we are closely looking at the nature right next to us, and deduct from our observations what can cause healing and maintain health. We build the scientific knowledge on the efficient ancestral multicultural solutions.
