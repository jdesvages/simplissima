Alexandra Caude (Hyperlink to Bio ACaude2-AHC.docx), our founder is a scientist, a professor of university, in addition to a mother of five. As such, we place a high value on making our science simple and accessible to all.

SimplissimA follows a pragmatic path into fighting diseases, educating and promoting cultural transmission. We gather and apply both the theoretical and practical knowledge in everyday life questions and challenges.

We invite potential actors to discuss and brainstorm at our café Jomo, for ‘Joy of Missing Out’. JoMo is an open, interactive platform for those who want to identify and share practical solutions for our health and our environment. We take into consideration:

  • the natural history of people,
  • the environment of populations,
  • the changes in the lifestyle,
  • the indications of ancestral remedies.
